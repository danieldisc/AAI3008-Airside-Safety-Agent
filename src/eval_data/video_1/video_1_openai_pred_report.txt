### OVERALL INCIDENT SUMMARY

During the footage, an individual repeatedly violated airside safety protocols by entering and remaining in a hazardous area near an active aircraft propeller. The person was observed directly interacting with the spinning propeller, disregarding established exclusion and danger zones.

### COMBINED NARRATIVE SEQUENCE

The sequence begins with a person approaching and standing dangerously close to an aircraft with an active propeller. Over the frames, the individual touches the plane near the propeller, completely disregarding the 7.5-meter exclusion zone and the 5-meter ingestion hazard zone. Despite the propeller being active, the individual continues to remain within this high-risk area for an extended period. Subsequently, even when the propeller is inactive, they continue to breach the exclusion area, indicating a sustained disregard for safety protocols.

### RULE REFERENCE & VERDICT

The individual violated the following Changi Airside Safety Directives:
1. **Exclusion Zones**: Failure to maintain a 7.5-meter buffer from an active propeller.
2. **Ingestion Hazard**: Breach of the 5-meter radius suction zone.
3. **Jet Blast**: Although not applicable here, the disregard for general safety distances signifies a systemic issue.

**Final Status**: VIOLATION

The ongoing violations suggest a potential lack of awareness or a wilful disregard for airside safety protocols.
